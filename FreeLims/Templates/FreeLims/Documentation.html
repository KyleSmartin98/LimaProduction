Getting Started

1.1 About

Welcome to LIMa ( Lab Inventory Management application), this software is meant to more easily connect analysts to their environment and samples. LIMa is a powerful tool created by a former QC analyst for analysts, technicians, and leads.

1.2 Creating an Account

If you are first accessing LIMa through your browser you will need to navigate the Sign up page via the link at the top right corner of the landing page.

Once the Registration form has appeared you will need to input a valid:

  1. Alpha-neumeric Username (LIMa suggests using a FirstNameLastNameEmployeeId structure)
  2. Organization (LIMa suggests using your corporate organization or location code and any unique alpha-neumeric characters)
    • Each additional user added to an organization will need to input the same Organization as the initial user
  3. Email
  4. Password (LIMa recommends an alpha-neumeric password of at least 8 characters and one special character)
Upon the completion of your registration, you will receive an email with information regarding your account. If you do not receive an email, you can change your email address as described in section 2.2.3.1.1: Update Personal Information. Additionally, you will need to request another Secret Key as described in section 2.2.3.1.3: Request a Secret Key.

1.3 Logging In

In order to sign in to your account you will need to input the username and password you created at the time of signing up for a LIMa account. If you forget your password you can create a new one in accordance with section 1.4: Resetting Your Password.

1.4 Resetting Your Password

If you have forgotten your password or your password is no longer valid to your account you may reset your password using the following link: Link to Reset. In order to reset your password you will need to use the secret key that was emailed to you when your account was registered. Without your secret key you will be unable to reset your password.
