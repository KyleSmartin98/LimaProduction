Features

Sample & Reagent Inventory Management

Records can easily be scaled to your lab's individual testing volume. Including sample record generation, testing assignment and result reporting. Reagent inventory can be easily created for multiple use-case scenarios allowing your lab to accurately track consumption, stock and expiry.

Barcode & Report Generation

Each sample or reagent entry automatically generates a barcode which can be used for inventory identification within the lab. Additionally, for each inventory type a CSV report can be downloaded allowing your lab further control over your data.

Inventory Expiry Notification

Not only does GlabaLIMS track your lab inventory, but alerts you of all samples and reagents within 30 days of their expiry on the homepage. Thus, Reducing the need to manually check inventory or wait for email reports like other LIMS applications

Data Trending & Method Storage*

Currently *premium features Data Trending & Method Storage packages allow your lab to more easily integrate otherwise external services into a single application. Allowing your analysts less downtime searching for methods or trending results. Please note, GlobaLIMS is not a raw data analysis or document control application.
